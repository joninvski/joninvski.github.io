Academic Career

João Paulo Trindade graduated in Computer and Informatics Engineering (2002-2007) by the Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) of the Universidade Técnica de Lisboa, where we is currently a PhD student, under the supervision of Professor Teresa Vazão.

His current interests include routing, network management and Mobile Ad-Hoc Networks.
